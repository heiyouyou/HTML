参观须知

1.禁止向动物投掷石块、食物和其他物品。Do not throw stones, food or other objects at animals.

2.禁止恐吓动物。Do not scare or tease animals.

3.欲上到顶层参观的游客应排队上楼，以免发生危险。Please queue up to get to the top floor. Do not crowd.

4.自觉遵守上述规定，以确保游客与动物的安全。Please abide by the above regulations to ensure the safety of both visitors and animals.
